2. Pricing

All prices quoted are fixed for the agreed route and vehicle. Prices exclude VAT unless stated otherwise. Any additional stops, waiting time beyond the complimentary period, or changes to the agreed route may incur additional charges, which will be communicated in advance.

3. Payment

Payment is due as agreed at the time of booking. We accept card payments via Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Full payment is required prior to travel unless a corporate account with invoice terms is in place.

4. Cancellations & Amendments

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the journey price. No-shows are non-refundable. Amendments to bookings are subject to availability and must be notified as early as possible.

5. Flight Monitoring & Waiting Time

For airport arrivals, we monitor your flight in real time and adjust your pickup accordingly. We provide 60 minutes of complimentary waiting time from the actual landing time. Additional waiting time is chargeable.
