Our Company

Over two decades of excellence in executive travel and premium chauffeur services across the United Kingdom.

Who We Are

Aurex Executive Travel is a premier chauffeur company providing reliable, luxurious, and discreet transportation services across the UK. With a strong commitment to punctuality, comfort, and professionalism, we cater to corporate executives, business travellers, and private clients who demand the very highest standards in ground transportation.

Our journey began with a single luxury vehicle and a vision to provide the business community with transportation services that matched the highest international standards. Today, we operate a fleet of premium Mercedes-Benz and electric vehicles, serving thousands of satisfied clients across the UK each year.

Our Promise

Every journey is meticulously planned. We monitor your flight in real time, meet you in the arrivals hall with a personalised name board, and handle your luggage — so you arrive relaxed. Our fixed-price model means you always know the cost before you travel. No hidden charges, no surprises.

With over 20 years of experience, we have built our reputation on three core principles: reliability, luxury, and exceptional customer service.
